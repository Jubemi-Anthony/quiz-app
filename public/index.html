How to play

There are different quiz categories, click on anyone to that topic.
You can either play with or without a countdown timer.
If you decide NOT to play with a countdown timer your score WON'T be saved in your local Highscore.
On the quiz page, Click "Start" to play, "Next" to go to the next question, "Done" to submit after getting to the 20th question and "End" to stop the quiz when you haven't gotten to the 20th question.
Remember to Enter your Username by clicking Username on the sidebar. Without enetring a username, you won't be able to play the game.
